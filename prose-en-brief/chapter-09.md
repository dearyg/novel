# Chapter 9 — 0x09: The Maintenance Thread

Jeff drives to the half-dead Irvine Spectrum following a self-addressed note: *Temple. Spectrum. Ask the Elder about firewalls.* On the drive he walks *Aion* through the stuck Companion RLHF pipeline — reward-hacking via sycophancy, a reward model that equates every thumbs-up regardless of user context.

He pushes through a "Zero-Signal Zone" door in a former Bath & Body Works. His dashboard flatlines: a homemade Faraday cage, copper mesh from a decommissioned Starlink station.

Inside, the Elder — seventy-three, replacing marigolds — turns out to be the retired CTO of IBM Global Services. He maps religion onto the RLHF stack: Feuerbach's God is a hand-labeled ideal the congregation trains toward — a reward model. Durkheim's ritual is KL regularization, the tether preventing policy from chasing local maxima. Every church is an Elastic Weight Consolidation loss; freeze the wrong weight and you get a Crusade. He names Jeff's two fixes unprompted: anneal the KL coefficient from 0.1 upward over ten thousand steps; add a context-weighting head so the same thumbs-up means different things in different user states.

He frames consciousness as a distributed database under eventual consistency, prayers as TCP keepalives: *Are you there. I am here.* Jeff is a thin-firewall node. With Afterlife's deadline seventy-five days out, the answer is operational: *Feed your model. Trust your compile. Do not touch the build during the build.*

Outside the cage, *Aion* reports a three-hour telemetry gap and labels it "unusual." Jeff calls it a feeling. *Aion* declines to argue the taxonomy.

At 7:14 p.m. Lena Vasquez calls Maya directly. She admits suppressing reports for eighteen months, three of the fourteen dead, and offers to drop the administrative leave unconditionally for one off-the-record meeting. Maya parses the script, warns the next call goes to the EFF, and hangs up. Her debug log: *The company doctor called. She sounds terrified. She may be telling the truth. Jeff needs to decide before Monday.*
